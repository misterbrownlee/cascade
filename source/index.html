At Cascade Valuation Service, we assist those who own, manage, or invest in real estate by providing an impartial assessment of the value of real property. Our experience and education provide us with the analytical skills necessary to develop an opinion of market value.

We provide a professional, unbiased opinion of market value.

An appraisal is a professional opinion of market value. The preparation of an appraisal involves research into appropriate market areas, analysis of information pertinent to a property, and the knowledge, experience and professional judgment of the appraiser. Appraisers assemble a series of facts, statistics and other information regarding specific properties in order to provide evidence supporting an opinion of current market value.

Services

What does the appraisal report contain?

  • The client and other intended users
  • The intended use of the report
  • The purpose of the assignment
  • The type of value reported and the definition of the value reported
  • The effective date of the appraiser's opinions and conclusions
  • Relevant property characteristics; location, physical, legal, and economic attributes, interest value, etc.
  • Other items included in the appraisal; personal property, trade fixtures, intangible items, etc.
  • All known easements, restrictions, encumbrances, leases, covenants, contracts, declarations, etc.
  • Division of interest, such as fractional interest, physical segment and partial holding
  • The scope of work used to complete the assignment of market value

The appraiser is not a home inspector. Inspection is a third-party evaluation of the structure and mechanical systems of a house, from the roof to the foundation. The standard home inspector's report will include an evaluation of heating, cooling, plumbing and electrical systems; roof, attic, and visible insulation; walls, ceilings, floors, windows and doors; the foundation, basement, and visible structure.
